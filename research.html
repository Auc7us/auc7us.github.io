Research & Work Summary

I focus on real-time ML systems, embedded AI, and autonomy—building systems that run efficiently on resource-constrained edge devices. My work spans perception pipelines, LLM compression, multi-agent collaboration, and high-fidelity simulation for autonomous systems.

Interests: Real-Time ML Systems, Embedded AI, 3D Perception, Embodied AI, VLMs, LLMs, NLP, Runtime Optimization, GenAI.
